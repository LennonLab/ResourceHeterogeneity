Title: Digital Water Atlas of Michigan
Description:
Abstract:
This dataset was released by James Breck, Michigan Department of Natural Resources, at the Institute for Fisheries Research. The statewide Michigan Hydrography Framework (version?) (based upon TIGER/Line Files, including basemap features based on both MDNR MIRIS files and enhanced linear referencing system built from the Michigan Accident Location Index (MALI)) was utilized as the basis of this dataset. Lake polygons present in this layer were edited (shape, attributes, location, duplicate representations for a lake in multiple counties), created for lakes that were detemined to be missing, and adjusted to reflect our current knowledge of the waterbodies throughout the state. For each lake (~10 acres or larger), a "New-Key" was assigned, based upon Humphrys and Green (1962). This unique identifier serves to link these saptial representations to numerous datasets (historical, current, and future) containing information pertaining to water quality, depth, origin, etc..
Purpose:
This dataset was developed from the Michigan Hydro framework to represent our most current information on lake area and location. It is intended for inclusion in the Digital Water Atlas of Michigan (based upon the 1:100K NHD). It is for use by state agency employees and individuals for pursuits related to the improvment of inland lake information or those assessing the state of water quality and aquatic resources in Michigan.

Logical_Consistency_Report:
These lake features present in the Michigan Hydrologic Framework (version ?) were included as the basemap. Since acquiring this spatial dataset in 199????, many edits were made to both the spatial representation and attributes. Largely, this information, shares the consistency of the Michigan Hydrologic Framework. While improving the framework's spatial representation and attributes, a "New_Key" was added to each lake polygon so that it could be linked to other sources of data, namely the database of MI lakes compiled here at the Institute for Fisheries Research.

Process_Description:
